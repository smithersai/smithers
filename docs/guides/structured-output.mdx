This material is now in [How It Works](/how-it-works#tasks-three-modes).

Schema-validation failures trigger up to 2 in-attempt repair prompts (with the Zod errors appended) before the attempt itself fails — so `retries={2}` effectively gives you up to 9 chances at valid JSON. `runId`, `nodeId`, and `iteration` are stripped from agent responses before validation and auto-populated on write.
